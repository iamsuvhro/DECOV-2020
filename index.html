   Health Care

  

Problem Code Problem Statement Details
CTH101 Predicting the next hotspot so that the spread could be contained. WE ALL ARE AMIDST A PANDEMIC SITUATION WHERE THE WHOLE WORLD IS SUFFERING FROM A DEADLY VIRUS WHICH HAS COST US A LOT OF LIVES. OUR GOVERNMENT IS CONSTANTLY PUTTING THEIR EFFORT TO FIND OUT THE NUMBER OF PEOPLE AFFECTED IN AN AREA AND MARKING THE MOST AFFECTED AREAS AS THE HOTSPOTS. BUT AT THE SAME TIME THE GOVERNMENT IS ALSO FACING A MOST CRUCIAL PROBLEM AND THAT IS PREDICTING THE NEXT MOST AFFECTED AREA. FIND A SOLUTION TO TACKLE THE PROBLEM EFFICIENTLY.
CTH102 How to spread awareness and precautionary measures to every section of society? IN A COUNTRY WITH THE SECOND HIGHEST POPULATION IN THE WORLD, THE MOST IMPORTANT THING TO DO RIGHT NOW IS TO SPREAD AWARENESS AMONG THE MAXIMUM NUMBER OF PEOPLE, NOT ONLY ABOUT COVID’19 BUT ALSO TO MAINTAIN THEIR HEALTH AND IMMUNITY IN THIS LOCKDOWN. SUGGEST A SOLUTION TO SPREAD AWARENESS AND THE PRECAUTIONARY MEASURES RELATED TO COVID’19.
CTH103 Virtual Consultation with the doctor should be promoted so that crowds can be avoided. THE INITIATIVE TAKEN BY THE GOVERNMENT TO FIGHT COVID’19 IS A GREAT ONE BUT ALSO DUE TO THIS, ALL THE DOCTORS GOT BUSY WITH TREATING PEOPLE FIGHTING WITH CORONAVIRUS. AT THE SAME TIME WE SHOULD ALSO NOT IGNORE THE PATIENTS WITH SEVERAL OTHER DISEASES. DUE TO THE LOCKDOWN PEOPLE ARE FACING PROBLEMS TO FIND AN APPOINTMENT FOR THEIR DOCTORS. FIND A SOLUTION SO THAT THOSE PATIENTS CAN VIRTUALLY CONSULT THEIR DOCTORS AND GET BACK TO THEIR MEDICATIONS.
CTH104 How about e-prescriptions and keeping medical records online in order to avoid physical contact AS WE CAN SEE, THE VIRUS TRANSMITS FROM PERSON TO PERSON. WITH THE INCREASED NUMBER OF PATIENTS, IT IS GETTING DIFFICULT FOR THE DOCTORS TO KEEP RECORDS OF MORE THAN ONE PATIENT AT A TIME. ON THE OTHER HAND, THE DOCTORS ALSO NEED TO MONITOR THE SYMPTOMS OCCURRING IN THE PATIENTS.COME UP WITH A SOLUTION SO THAT THE DOCTORS CAN EFFICIENTLY KEEP RECORDS AND TREAT THE PATIENTS.

Rules & Regulations

  • Team members should be at least 3 and max 5.

  • Teams should describe what they’ve made and that should be audible not necessary person in on screen.

  • Only UG and PG Students can join.

First Round ( 1st may to 23rd may ) :

Registration of teams they can select at most 3 statements . . send ppts. Selection will be based on their ideas and ppts . 24th may - 25th may : selected teams will get notified through mail.

 Second Round ( 27th  may 10.00 pm - 29 th may 10 p.m) (Online) :

  • 27th may - keynote speakers ( 8 p.m - 10 p.m)

  • After completing their apps teams need to create a video what they made upload it to youtube that should be public and teams will upload their code to github.
